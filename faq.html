FAQ
Find answers to frequently asked questions
What is QNS?
QNS is the native token of QuantoSwap – Ethereum-based DEX. The token allows you to earn income from staking, farming, and also through the referral program. For completing tasks in the application, you will earn QNS and can exchange it for other tokens on the exchange.
How to mine QNS?
Just tap on your phone's screen, easy & free. But you can also increase the number of rewards in QNS by upgrading and completing tasks in the 'Tasks' section.
How to get more QNS?
To get more QNS, go to the 'Mine' section and purchase various upgrades. They will allow you to earn more QNS per hour. You can also perform regular tasks in the 'Tasks' section: provide liquidity to the exchange, stake, farm, invite friends, etc.
Are there reward limits on QuantoSwap?
You can earn an infinite amount of QNS by tapping and completing tasks. You can also receive 10% of the rewards of each of your referrals. You are not limited in any way in the amount of QNS you can earn.
How to withdraw tokens?
To withdraw tokens, go to Home and click Deposit/Withdraw in the top right corner. Select the 'Withdraw' function and withdraw from 0.1 QNS to your ERC20 address. The funds will be credited to your account when the transaction is completed.
How to increase profit per hour?
Go to the 'Mine' section and purchase an upgrade for your profile. Some upgrades are available immediately, while others are only available upon reaching a certain level.
